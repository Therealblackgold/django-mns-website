About Us

East Rand based Macmillan & Sibusiso Innovations founded by two childhood friends Thabo Macmillan Maja & Lebohang Sibusiso Ncholo, after many years of building their own individual brands as freelance workers in their own respective fields Macmillan as tiles, floors, construction expert and Lebohang as a third generation carpenter of his family with over two decades experience. Inspired by a common goal skills and experience Macmillan & Sibusiso Innovations was born.

What we do

Our commercial projects include Carpentry/Woodworking, Tiling, new Handyman, construction and repairs/restorations. Occupied and fully operational job sites are never a problem. And we can also plan, manage, and build multi-phase jobs.

Tiling projects

Tiles can give a room a whole new look. You might be ripping up old floors and putting in new tile flooring, or transforming a bathroom with fresh walls and floors.
Whatever the project, tiles are a unique way to bring personality to your home.
We specialise in tiling: from shower floors to external tiling, cladding and bathrooms. Whatever your tiling needs, we have the skills and experience to bring your vision to life.
